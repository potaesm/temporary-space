Allianz History

Allianz AG was founded in Berlin on 5 February 1890 by then director of the Munich Reinsurance Company Carl von Thieme (a native of Erfurt, whose father was the director of Thuringia) and Wilhelm von Finck (co-owner of the Merck Finck & Co bank). The joint company was listed in Berlin’s trade register under the name Allianz Versicherungs-Aktiengesellschaft. The first Allianz products were marine and accident policies first sold only in Germany, however in 1893 Allianz opened its first international branch office in London. It distributed marine insurance coverage to German clientele looking for coverage abroad.

Allianz Group started operating in Asia in 1910.  At present, Allianz Group sees very high business growth in Asia, with customer base of over 21 million in 16 countries including Thailand.

One of Allianz Ayudhya’s major shareholders is Allianz Group, the world’s leading financial group, making Allianz Ayudhya an established, strong, and reliable insurance company.

Allianz Ayudhya – Thailand’s leading insurance company

Allianz Group started operating in Thailand in 1951.  At present, Allianz Ayudhya consists of 2 core businesses namely Allianz Ayudhya Assurance Pcl. offering life and health insurance, and Allianz Ayudhya General Insurance Pcl. offering non-life insurance.  Both businesses provide comprehensive protection for all lifestyles’ needs.

We are ready to serve our customers with over 1400 employees and 27 branches and service center, plus over 200 AZAY agencies nationwide (data as of July 2020).
